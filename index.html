Leon Joanknecht - About me

When I was 17 years old I discovered photography using my fathers analog Pentax, wandering through Venice. I have been hooked ever since.

At 18 years old I spend some time in Nepal, a country with breathtaking people, culture and landscapes. Its was the perfect learning environment! With a photo from Nepal I won first prize at the Algemeen Dagblad Photo Contest 2007, and after that I started doing more photography projects.

In 2010 I attended the Advanced Photography Course of the SKVR, Rotterdam. This year I am participating in the National Geographic Photography Masterclass 2022.
